About Us

Shikshadeep Hostel is one of the well managed hostel.A good private hostel offers a comfortable, secure, and convenient environment for guests, with additional amenities and facilities to enhance the overall experience.

Our Facilities

Comfortable Rooms

• Private Rooms: Clean and well-maintained private rooms with comfortable beds, quality linens, and pillows.

• Adequate Space: Rooms should be spacious enough to accommodate personal belongings with sufficient storage space (e.g., closets, drawers).

• Air Conditioning/Heating: Climate control for comfort in different weather conditions.

• Soundproofing: To ensure privacy and minimal noise disruptions..

Security and Privacy

• Secure Lockers: Lockers in each room or common area to store valuable items safely.

• Private Bathroom Options: Many private hostels offer en-suite bathrooms for added convenience and privacy.

• Night-time Security: Security staff available during the night to ensure safety.

Cleanliness and Hygiene

• Regular Cleaning: Daily cleaning of private rooms, common areas, and bathrooms to maintain high hygiene standards.

• Fresh Linens and Towels: Clean towels and linens provided regularly, or upon request..

• Waste Management: Proper disposal of waste, including segregated bins for recyclables and general trash.

Comfortable Sleeping Arrangements

• Comfortable beds with quality mattresses, pillows, and clean bedding.

• Quiet environment conducive to sleep, with noise control measures.

• Adequate space in rooms (not overcrowded).
